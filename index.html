📈 Sentiment Analysis & Short-Term Return Predictability in Small-Cap Nuclear Equities — MSc Thesis (EDHEC, 2026)

Does FinBERT-derived news sentiment improve short-term return prediction for small-cap nuclear equities? This MSc thesis builds a fully reproducible five-phase pipeline — feasibility audit, data engineering (3,992 ticker-days, 25,885 news articles covering SMR, LEU, NNE), FinBERT sentiment scoring, LSTM training with chronological hold-out, and SHAP explainability. Key finding: sentiment augmentation yields a statistically significant average MAE improvement of +5.18% at the 5-day horizon; no aggregate benefit at the 1-day horizon. Hypothesis testing via Diebold-Mariano tests.
